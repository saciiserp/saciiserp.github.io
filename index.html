IISER Pune at the Akhil Bhartiya Shiksha Samagam 2023

SAC team showcasing hands-on activities to Honorable Prime Minister of India Shri Narendra Modi

IISER Pune participated at the Akhil Bharatiya Shiksha Samagam (ABSS), held on July 29-30, 2023, in New Delhi. This event was jointly organized by the Ministry of Education and the Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship as part of the 3rd Anniversary of the National Education Policy - 2020.

The Honorable Prime Minister of India, Shri Narendra Modi, who addressed the inaugural session at the event, visited the IISER Pune stall, where SAC members demonstrated a few activities that can be incorporated into the curriculum for better teaching and learning outcomes.

About 7,000 visitors, mainly school students and teachers, visited IISER Pune's stall during the event. Many students and educators got an opportunity to try out some of the science toys displayed at the stall.

CPSE Roundtable and Exhibition 2023

SAC team at CPSE Exhibition 2023

On September 25-26, the Outreach and Endowment team from IISER Pune had participated in Central Public Sector Enterprises (CPSE) Roundtable & Exhibition 2023, hosted by the Department of Public Enterprises, Govt. of India, at Pragati Maidan, New Delhi.

Prof. M S Santhanam (Dean IR&O) and Shubhangi Wankhede (PTO, Endowment & Outreach) at CPSE Exhibition 2023

Prof. M S Santhanam (Dean IR&O) and Shubhangi Wankhede (PTO, Endowment & Outreach) engaged in the CPSEs Roundtable conclave, focusing on Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) with a special emphasis on aspirational districts. They interacted with CPSEs delegates from ONGC, GAIL, NTPC, and other PSUs, emphasizing the potential of STEM education as a CSR initiative.
